Welcome to Wilhelmina Legacy Tribute

This is the home page of my tribute of my grandmother Wilhelmina, whoever called Mina.

Wilhelmina was born in 1941 in a small town in the gulf regional area of Louisiana. She lived through significant historical events, including World War II, and was known for her resilience and kindness. She had a passion for gardening and cooking, often sharing her delicious recipes with family and friends. Wilhelmina's legacy lives on through her children and grandchildren, who cherish the memories and values she instilled in them.

She passed away in December of 2009, but her spirit continues to inspire those who knew her. This tribute page is dedicated to celebrating her life and the impact she had on her loved ones.
